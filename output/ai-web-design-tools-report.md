# Top 3 AI Tools for Web Design in 2026

## 1. Figma

Figma has integrated AI capabilities that help with smart layout suggestions, auto-tidying components, and quickly generating variant screens for responsive breakpoints. The platform allows designers to explore how a homepage adapts across mobile, tablet, and desktop in just a few minutes using AI-assisted design workflows. Figma Sites launched a CMS in public beta in November 2025, closing a major gap with competitors, and now ships with preset interactions including marquee scrolling, custom cursors, hover effects, and parallax, with the ability to generate custom interactions from AI prompts.

## 2. Framer

Framer targets creatives, agencies, and designers who want AI to handle the grunt work while they focus on aesthetics, generating layouts based on design principles like hierarchy, white space, and visual flow. The platform provides controls for precise spacing, custom animations, and advanced typography settings after initial AI generation. Framer doubles down on generative AI with Text-to-Site, creating animated landing pages in minutes that you can publish as live websites without needing to code.

## 3. PlayCode

PlayCode is the best all-in-one AI web design tool in 2026, working like a premium design agency that clarifies your ideas, creates wireframes, and generates complete websites using 15+ AI models. It lets you click anything to edit it visually, combining generative capabilities with an intuitive visual editor for refining designs on the spot. PlayCode eliminates the blank canvas problem by providing AI-generated, multi-screen mockups that are production-ready and fully editable.
